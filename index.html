About

A journey back to the beginning

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Being young and inexperienced, we started working in this industry in various capacities within one of Malaysia's largest home & living retailer learning the different skills of the trade.

  • 2011-2020

    A Strong Friendship is Born

    Despite us leaving our positions in the industry to pursue our own interests, our friendship remained. We would often meet and reminiscence about our time working together.

  • March 2020

    Ad Hoc Services

    As fate would have it, people who knew we were previously working in the home furnishing business enquired if we still had contacts in the industry. We gathered together to perform an ad hoc job. The job was a success and we received many referrals from that job!

  • September 2021

    Business Registration

    With some successful projects under our belts. We decided it was time to formalise our business and introduce the world to CS CURTAIN!

Main Living Area, Bungalow House

Grand, full ceiling height double layer rod pocket.

Our client wanted a grand feeling in the main living area. For this we recommended full ceiling height curtains. The curtain boxes were already prepared and a double layer comprising sheer and blackout curtains would allow day and night variation choosing between privacy and cheerful sunshine.

  • Client: Mr Tan
  • Location: Bukit Jalil

Family Area, Bungalow

Cosy, comfortable feel using plain eyelets.

Our client wanted a simple cosy area for the family to gather. Nothing too grand or gaudy. Plain coloured curtains matched the neutral coloured walls and furniture. Client liked the eyelet curtain rods.

  • Client: Mr. Edward
  • Location: Puchong

Dining Area, Bungalow

Practical use, simple design

Our client did not plan to install curtains initially due to the beautiful high double volume ceiling. The sunlight entering the dining area however appeared too bright at times. Client decided on plain coloured double volume, single layer, rod pocket curtains to maintain the traditional styling.

  • Client: Mr Lim
  • Location: Rawang Golf Resort

Living Area, Condo
